Here are few frequently asked questions about YMCA Stock Exchange

Game is very simple.

Every user is provided with initial balance of 50,000 INR. You are allowed to buy shares from any of the 10 available companies from Profile TAB.

Every user will have access to Stats TAB before the start of the game. This page contains every detail user want to know about the companies that are available in the market. These details will help user to analyse company's performance in future.

Trading can be done from 21th March to 27th March.

Trading on shares can be done from 10 AM to 11 PM on specified days.

Since real trading has many other options we have kept it simple for you. You can buy Shares and Sell them. Now its up to you how you analyse the trend and bet on which company.

For Your convinience we have provided the facility of Loan so that you can buy more shares than usual.

Loan is extra money that you can avail in case u see a very profitable share you want to buy.

Loan is easy to access. You can request for loan from your Profile TAB. Just fill in the required amount and it will be deducted from your available loan amount. But make sure to repay loan in time.

Every loan purchase lead to deduction of a small amount of cash from your original balance. So make sure to not request for loan when not needed as it can lead to your dedudction of your hard earned money.

Choosing winner is very difficult tast. Primarily winner would be anyone who has maximum amount of cash balance available at the end of the game.

This means that anyone who has taken loan from the system, that will be first repayed, intrest would be deducted and then the final balance will be calculate

Home TAB is main page of the application. It does the most important task of guiding the user. Some of its tasks are :

# It shows the Zen Index of the YMCA Stock Exchange.

# It shows important dates and timings of game.

Zensex is Zen Index of YMCA Stock Exchange. It shows the cumlative price of top few companies registered with YSE.

Zensex gives you the idea of how frequently the trading prices are changing.

Market TAB tells user about all the things they want to know about a company's stock prices.

It basically shows the current share price per share of company. It also tells about change in price of company share price since last trade on that share.

It also tell about company's day's highest and lowest price from start of trading hour to end of trading hour.

Profile TAB is present to do all the main things.

# You can Buy Shares of any company.

# You can Sell your bought Shares.

# You can Apply for Loan.

# You can Pay your Loan Back.

# Last but not the least. You can watch profit on your shares in real time.(You have to wait for some time to let it auto refresh)

Stats TAB takes you to a literally new TAB where it shows you all the details you want to know about your favorite company.

You can compare the Trading Charts of each company and even make tactics for future trading.

Leaderboard TAB shows you the current position of all the players registered in the system.

This tab not only shows you the current blance of each player but also shows their Loan to be repayed, which makes it possible to analyse who gonna end up where.

Transactions TAB does one thing. It shows all of your previous Selling and Buying Tasks to You. This Info is personnal as noone can see it except you.

Even if someone manages to see your previous transactions ,then also it won't make any difference to you or to him.
